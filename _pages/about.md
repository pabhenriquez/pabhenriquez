I am an Associate Professor at the [Facultad de Administracion y Economia](http://economiayempresa.udp.cl/), Universidad Diego Portales, Santiago, Chile. I am also co-Director of the Master in Business Analytics and Data Science, and Director of the Master in Digital Business and Data Analytics (Full time) and the Master in Digital Business.

I received my B.Sc. (2012), P.E. and M.Sc. (2015) degrees in Physics from Universidad de Concepcion, Chile, and my Ph.D. (2019) in Complex Systems Engineering from Universidad Adolfo Ibanez, Chile.

My research interests include **machine learning**, **data science**, **business intelligence**, **data mining**, **deep learning** and **neural networks with random weights**.

I am also co-founder of [Whitebox S.A.](https://thewhitebox.cl/es/), a data analytics consulting firm.
