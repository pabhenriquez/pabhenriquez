I received my B.Sc. (2012), P.E. and M.Sc. (2015) degrees in Physics from Universidad de Concepcion, Concepcion, Chile. I completed my Ph.D. degree (2019) in Complex Systems Engineering at Universidad Adolfo Ibanez, Chile. Currently, I am an Associate Professor at the Facultad de Administracion y Economia, Universidad Diego Portales, Santiago, Chile. I am also co-Director of the Master in Business Analytics and Data Science, and Director of the Master in Digital Business and Data Analytics (Full time) and the Master in Digital Business.

My research interests include machine learning, data science, business intelligence, data mining, deep learning and neural networks with random weights.

Academic Editor, PLOS ONE, 2023-present.

Academic Editor, PLOS Complex Systems, 2024-present.
